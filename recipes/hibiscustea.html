Fizzy Hibiscus Tea

What is it?

A tangy, refreshing drink that's perfect for hot summer days.

So, what do you need?

  • Hibiscus tea bags
  • Club soda
  • Fresh limes
  • Honey or agave syrup
  • Fresh mint

How do you make it?

  1. Brew the hibiscus tea. You'll want at least 4 cups of tea.
  2. Put brewed tea in the fridge. Chill overnight.
  3. When your ready to enjoy, fill a tall glass half way with the tea. Top it off with the soda.
  4. Add a teaspoon or two of fresh lime juice.
  5. Add honey or agave syrup to taste.
  6. Garnish with mint and enjoy!
