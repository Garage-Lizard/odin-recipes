Zesty Tajin Corn

So, what is it?

This recipe is a no-fuss sidedish that's perfect with a smokey or spicy main dish.

What do you need?

  • Fresh corn
  • Butter
  • Tajin

How do you make it?

  1. Shuck and cook the ears of corn. You can gril them or steam them. Totally up to you.
  2. Once the corn is cooked, melt about 1 tablespoon of butter.
  3. Take the corn off the grill or out of the steam pot. Put on a heat safe plate and brush all sides with the butter.
  4. Sprinkly Tajin to taste. Don't be afraid to pile it on.
  • Alternative method: If you don't like to eat corn off the cob, carefully slice the kernals off the cob with a chopping knife.
    Put the corn in a bowl, and then mix in the butter and Tajin.
